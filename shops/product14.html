Sunny Weather

Hot weather, India

Rs. 2000

The painting captures a serene and contemplative scene set in bright, sunny weather. In the center of the composition, there's a tree that stands bare, devoid of leaves, its branches reaching out in stark contrast to the clear, blue sky. Perched on one of these branches is a solitary bird, adding a touch of life and movement to the otherwise still and quiet landscape. The tree, without its leaves, seems to symbolize the changing of seasons, possibly the transition from autumn to winter. Its branches, while appearing barren, also exude a sense of strength and resilience in their simplicity.
