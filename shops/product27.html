Snow Mountains

White Painting, India

Rs. 2000

The painting portrays a breathtaking and serene scene of a snow-covered mountain landscape. This artwork captures the pristine beauty and awe-inspiring majesty of snow-capped peaks. The dominant color in the painting is white, with varying shades and textures to depict the snow. The artist skillfully renders the mountain range, conveying the ruggedness of the terrain and the way snow has transformed it into a winter wonderland. The interplay of light and shadow on the snow-covered slopes adds depth and dimension to the composition.
